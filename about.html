SAMARTHYA.

A non-profit initiative. We make it happen.

Samarthya (Indian meaning: Support) is a Indian based initiative non-government entity operating on the International level (10 countries). The main idea behind this NGO is to support, plan and carry out boots-on-the-ground projects to accomplish our objectives. Our goal as an organization is to accomplish the following:

  1. Eradication of Unemployment
  2. Good Healthcare to every sector of society
  3. To maximise the literacy rate
